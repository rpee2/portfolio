When the Answers Aren't On Chegg

Role: Acting

Overview

I was part of a main cast in a small play using the Forum Theater format. In Forum Theater, the play would run once normally. After running once, the play would run again and the audience members are given the power to stop the performance at anytime to propose a change. The actors would improvise a new direction in the scene with the change. This encourages the audience to think of different perspectives and take part in the hypothetical situation as presented.
